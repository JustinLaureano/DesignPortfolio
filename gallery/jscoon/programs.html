Jonathan believes that the best results will come when you follow a fitness program that is personalized to you and your goals. Everybody is different in terms of fitness level, mobility, age, and reason for training, and therefore all require programs that differ from one another. Since there is no one program that is perfect for everybody, Jon has a consultation with every client before they start training to determine what type of program will best fit their needs.

In addition to a personal fitness regimen, Jon will provide you with personalized nutritional guidance for maximum results possible. A well balanced diet along with healthy sleeping patterns will provide you with energy you never thought you could have.

Jonathan can put together a comprehensive training program for just about anybody. Whether you need to get stronger and faster for the next football season or lose weight to fit into your favorite jeans, Jon can provide you with the recipe for success and the motivation to stick to it.
