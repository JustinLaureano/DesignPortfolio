  • Consultation

Setting up a consultation is a very important first step in reaching your fitness goals. A consultation allows you to meet Jon one on one and let him get to know about you and for you to ask any questions you need answered. During this consultation Jon will find out about your fitness level by performing a series of simple measurements so he can begin constructing a work out plan suited to you. Jon will also discuss your nutrition habits to determine a suitable nutritional guideline plan for you.

The last part of the consultation is where you and Jon begin scheduling your first training sessions together. Jon's flexible schedule allows a wide range of times so you can pick ones that best fit your busy schedule.

In order to schedule your consultation and start living healthier, e-mail Jonathan Schoonover at one of the e-mail links listed below and leave your contact information so Jon can reach you. You may also call Jon at the number listed below during daytime hours to schedule a consultation.
