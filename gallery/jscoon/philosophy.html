The philosophy Jonathan Schoonover follows is simple - hard work and commitment net results. Jon doesn't believe in the latest fitness gimmicks and neither should you. By following the exercise advice of Jon you will engage in various strength, flexibility, and mobility exercises that are proven to be the most effective to reach your goals. These exercises will push your muscles to their limits in order for you to see the results you want and give you a feeling of self-confidence you have never had before.

Jonathan's programs also consist of various cardiovascular exercises to to keep your body and heart in great shape. By improving your cardiovascular endurance you will notice increased energy in all your everyday activities and lower stress levels as well.

Just as important as exercising in a balanced fitness plan is warming up and cooling down. Every session with Jon will start with dynamic warm up drills designed to correct muscular imbalances, mobility, postural issues, and prepares the body for more intense work later in the workout. Jon's programs always conclude with a cool down consisting of gentle stretching and soft tissue work so that your muscles recover faster, feel less sore, and function more efficiently.

In addition to Jonathan's exceptional fitness advice, he also offers nutritional education and guidance to supplement your fitness program. Jon knows that you can't "out train" a bad diet and that proper nutrition is essential for optimal results. Jon will provide you with detailed information regarding caloric intake needs, food selection, and shopping guidelines so you can make educated decisions when it comes to eating.

By following the foundation Jonathan will lay down, you will be well on your way to a much healthier you.
