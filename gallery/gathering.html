Overview

The Gathering is a fine dining restaurant in Findlay, OH that I designed a website for. Inspiration for the website came from the building's interior & decor as well as the sophistication of their menu.

The website features a fully responsive layout and collapsable menus, as well as tabbed navigation to seperate different areas of the menus. It was also designed to support an online reservation form, which makes it easy for the guests to reserve a table.

Software Used

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Adobe Illustrator
  • HTML/CSS/JQuery
  • Bootstrap 3
