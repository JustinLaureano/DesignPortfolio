About

The Gathering is located in the heart of downtown Findlay & has been serving fine food & spirits since 2010.

Enjoy a meal in our elegant dining room, or head next door to our wine bar to try any of the hundreds of different wine offerings. Just upstairs is our bourbon lounge, the Anvil, which can accomodate large private parties & offers a full service bar as well.
