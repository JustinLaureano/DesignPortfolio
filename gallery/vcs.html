Overview

Vicious Cycle Studio (VCS) is a music recording studio based out of Bowling green, OH. This brand identity package was created to reflect the kind of music VCS records and the atmosphere the company name inspires.

This project was completed as a freelance project while I was still completing my degree at Owens Community College.
